A CHEESECAKE RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Four-8 ounce blocks of cream cheese
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs

RECIPE

  1. Mix the ingredients
  2. Add the sugar and vanilla extract
  3. Cool it in the frige
  4. Serve it with a drink of your choice enjoy!!!
